## ğŸŒ“ Light vs Dark Mode

### Light Mode Strategy:

- **Lightness**: 60-70% (readable on white)
- **Chroma**: 15-22% (vibrant but not harsh)
- **Foreground**: Near-white text (99%)

### Dark Mode Strategy:

- **Lightness**: 72-80% (gentle on eyes in darkness)
- **Chroma**: 13-20% (slightly reduced for comfort)
- **Foreground**: Near-black text (15%)
